## What is this project?

InstantEvent is a photo collection app built for in-person events like weddings, markets, birthday parties, and pop-up experiences. It lets your guests scan a QR code, snap or upload a photo right from their phone, and then sit back while you handle turning that photo into a physical keepsake — like a magnet or print.

As an organizer, you get a live dashboard where every guest photo appears the moment it's uploaded. You can track each photo through your fulfillment process and, when their order is ready, guests automatically receive an email notification — no manual follow-up needed.

**This app is for you if you are:**
- A photographer or vendor who creates photo magnets or prints at events
- A small event business running wedding activations, market booths, or private parties
- Anyone who wants a fast, organized way to collect photos from guests and track fulfillment — without the chaos of paper sign-in sheets or generic file-sharing links

Your guests don't need an account or an app. They just scan, upload, and go.

## How to Use It

### For Organizers

**Step 1 — Create your account**

Sign up with your email and password to get access to your organizer dashboard.

![Step 1 - Sign up screen](screenshots/step1-signup.png)

**Step 2 — Create a new event**

From your dashboard, click "Create Event." Give your event a name, set the date, and optionally add a welcome message for your guests. Once saved, your event is ready to go.

![Step 2 - Create event form](screenshots/step2-create-event.png)

**Step 3 — Share the QR code with your guests**

Open your event and find the QR code. Print it out, display it on a sign, or share it digitally — however works best for your event setup. Every guest who scans it will land directly on your event's upload page.

![Step 3 - Event detail page with QR code](screenshots/step3-qr-code.png)

**Step 4 — Watch photos come in live**

As guests upload their photos, they appear in real time on your event dashboard as polaroid-style cards. You'll see each guest's name and can track the status of their order.

![Step 4 - Live photo grid on dashboard](screenshots/step4-live-grid.png)

**Step 5 — Update statuses as you fulfill orders**

Use the status labels on each photo to track where things stand. Move photos through the workflow as you process and complete each order.

![Step 5 - Status labels on photo cards](screenshots/step5-status-workflow.png)

**Step 6 — Guests get notified automatically**

When you mark a photo as completed, the guest receives an automatic email letting them know their keepsake is ready. No manual messages needed.

![Step 6 - Automated email notification](screenshots/step6-email-notification.png)

---

### For Guests

**Step 1 — Scan the QR code**

Look for the QR code at the event — on a sign, a table card, or displayed on a screen. Scan it with your phone's camera to open the upload page. No app download required.

![Step 1 - Scanning QR code at event](screenshots/guest-step1-scan.png)

**Step 2 — Enter your name and email**

Type in your name and the email address where you'd like to receive your notification when your keepsake is ready.

![Step 2 - Guest info form](screenshots/guest-step2-info-form.png)

**Step 3 — Upload and crop your photo**

Choose a photo from your camera roll or take one right then. You'll be able to crop it into a square so it looks great as a magnet or print.

![Step 3 - Photo crop tool](screenshots/guest-step3-crop-tool.png)

**Step 4 — Submit and you're done!**

Tap the submit button and you're all set. You'll receive a confirmation, and when your keepsake is ready, you'll get an email letting you know.

![Step 4 - Upload confirmation screen](screenshots/guest-step4-confirmation.png)
